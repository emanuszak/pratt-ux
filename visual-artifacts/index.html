h2How does it work?

Let the paint work. We start with a vision in our heart, and we put it on canvas. Let's make some happy little clouds in our world. You have to make almighty decisions when you're the creator. Let your heart be your guide.

h3Step 1

image

Here's something that's fun. Use what you see, don't plan it. The least little bit can do so much.

h3Step 2

image

You create the dream - then you bring it into your world. This is probably the greatest thing to happen in my life - to be able to share this with you.

h3Step 3

image

If you don't like it - change it. It's your world. Isn't that fantastic? You can just push a little tree out of your brush like that. Any little thing can be your friend if you let it be.
